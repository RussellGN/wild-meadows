Honey

Wild Meadows honey comes from wild African bees which are not teamed. We follow a strict feeding process for our bees consisting of wildflower nectar producing pure honey.

Our pure honey does not have any added sweeteners, flavours or preservatives.

You can purchase a jar of pure honey directly from us! Remember that pure honey will typically crystallize over time, as the natural sugars in honey tend to separate. This is natural and does not affect the quality of the honey.
